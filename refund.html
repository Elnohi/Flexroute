Refund Policy

Effective 1 August 2026 · Last updated 21 July 2026

The short version

  • Try before you pay — every driver gets free routes before any charge.
  • Not working out within 14 days of your first payment? Full refund, no questions.
  • Cancel any time. You keep access until the period you already paid for runs out.
  • Charged by mistake, or twice? We refund it, whenever it happened.

What FlexRoute costs

PlanPriceRenews

Both are recurring subscriptions handled by Stripe. You're charged automatically at the start of each period until you cancel.

The 14-day window

Within 14 days of your first payment on a plan, email us and we'll refund it in full. You don't need to justify it — "it didn't help me" is a complete reason.

This covers your first payment on a plan, not every renewal. If you've been subscribed eight months and forget to cancel before month nine, that falls under the next section.

Renewals you didn't mean to pay

Caught it early

Contact us within 7 days of a renewal charge, having optimised no routes in that new period, and we'll refund it. Forgetting to cancel is a normal thing to do and we're not going to keep your money over it.

Caught it late

Past 7 days we'll cancel immediately so you aren't charged again, and consider a partial refund case by case. Ask — we'd rather sort it out than have you file a chargeback.

Always refunded, no time limit

  • Duplicate charges — billed twice for the same period.
  • Charged after cancelling.
  • Paid but never got access — if premium never unlocked, that's our bug, and you get your money back if we can't fix it.
  • Unauthorised charges — someone used your card without permission.

How to cancel

Email flexrouteapp@gmail.com from the address you used at checkout. We'll cancel it and confirm in writing. Cancelling stops future charges — it doesn't cut off time you've already paid for. Cancel a yearly plan in month three and you keep premium through month twelve.

How to request a refund

Email flexrouteapp@gmail.com with:

  • The email address you used at checkout
  • Roughly when you were charged
  • One line on what went wrong — helps us fix it for the next driver

We reply within 2 business days. Approved refunds go through Stripe to your original payment method and typically land in 5–10 business days, depending on your bank.

Before you file a chargeback: please email us first. Chargebacks take weeks, cost us a fee on top of the refunded amount, and usually end with the same result you'd have got by asking. There is no refund request we'll be annoyed by.

Your legal rights

This policy sits on top of your statutory rights — it doesn't replace them.

In the UK or EU, you have a 14-day right of withdrawal on digital services. Because FlexRoute gives access immediately, you consent to that immediate start at checkout — but we honour the 14-day window regardless, as described above.

In Quebec and elsewhere in Canada, provincial consumer protection legislation applies and nothing here limits it.

Changes

If this policy changes, the version in effect when you were charged governs that charge. We'll update the date at the top.
