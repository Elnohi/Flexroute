Shift Mode — Step by Step
Your whole shift,
one stop at a time.

The biggest time-saver isn’t the route optimization — it’s what happens after. Shift Mode eliminates the app-switching between every delivery.

HOW TO USE SHIFT MODE
1

Upload & Optimize

Take 3–4 overlapping screenshots of your Flex itinerary. Upload them and tap Optimize. Takes under 2 minutes before your shift.

2

Tap Start Shift Mode

Green button on the results screen. The app switches to a full-screen view showing your first stop with a large readable address and package number.

3

Navigate & Deliver

Tap Waze or Maps. Drive. Switch to Amazon Flex to scan and mark delivered. Come back to FlexRoute when done at the stop.

4

Tap Delivered — Next Stop

The next address slides in. Progress bar updates. Your shift progress saves automatically — phone locks and restarts don’t lose your place.

● Progress bar shows X of Y stops complete
● Elapsed time shown in the footer
● Tap “All Stops” to see the full list with checkmarks
● Back arrow returns to full route list without losing progress
