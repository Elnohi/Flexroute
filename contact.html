Try this first

These four cover most of what lands in the inbox, and each takes under a minute.

A stop won't geocode

Check that your Region is set correctly at the top of the app — a wrong region sends the lookup to the wrong country. Then try adding the city and postal code to the address.

Scanning missed addresses

Screenshot the itinerary list rather than photographing your screen, and keep the text upright and unobscured. You can always add any missed stop by typing it.

A stop looks like it's in the wrong place

Tap the 📍 verify link next to it. That opens the exact pin on Google Maps, so you can see where FlexRoute thinks the stop is. If the pin is wrong, send us the address.

You paid but premium isn't active

Make sure you're using the same email you entered at checkout. If it still won't unlock, email us with that address and we'll fix it manually.

Sending a bug report

Include these and there's a good chance it's fixed in one round instead of four:

  • Your phone and browser — e.g. "Pixel 7, Chrome" or "iPhone 13, Safari"
  • Whether you installed from Google Play or use it in a browser
  • The exact address or stop involved, if it's a routing or scanning issue
  • What you expected, and what happened instead
  • A screenshot, if you can
One thing we can't help with: anything about your delivery account, your blocks, your pay, or your standing with a delivery platform. FlexRoute is an independent tool with no connection to any of them and no access to your account. For those, contact the platform's own support.
